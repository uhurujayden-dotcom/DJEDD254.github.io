Who Am I

THE SOUND
OF EDD254

Born and raised in Kenya's coastal heartbeat of Mombasa, DJ EDD254 has spent over 8 years mastering the art of the mix. Known for seamless genre-blending and an electric connection with the crowd, every set is a journey that keeps dancefloors locked in.

From intimate rooftop sessions overlooking the Indian Ocean to headline slots at Nairobi's biggest festivals — the energy never drops. The mission: turn any space into an experience people talk about for years.
